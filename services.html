SERVICES

THE CONCIERGE

La Ponche's concierge service provides flight confirmations, restaurant reservations, massages, fitness, yoga, transfers, pick-ups, boat and car rentals, as well as health information, private tours, personalized picnics, museum privatization…

THE PETIT PIAGGIO

The Petit Piaggio has become a character of La Ponche. It allows you to reach the private parking lot at the hotel. Since its birth 70 years ago, the Ape Calessino has been an iconic vehicle derived from the Vespa. Today, it is back with a delightful vintage look, gracefully weaving its way through the streets of Saint-Tropez. On request it can drop you off in any part of the city…

VALET PARKING AND GARAGE

Hôtel La Ponche has the privilege of having a covered garage with 16 spaces and 24-hour surveillance near the hotel (one minute's walk) and is served by the Petit Piaggio. Valet parking is available 24 hours a day. A cleaning service is also provided by the hotel. Rate: 30€ per day.

BOAT RENTAL

We offer the possibility to rent a motorboat for the day with a skipper. For your stay at sea, Chef Thomas Danigo will create a personalized picnic just for you.
