THE APARTMENT

It is a private apartment open to the sea. 72 m2 that can accommodate 4 people thanks to a bedroom and a large sofa bed. A fully equipped kitchen opens onto the living room, creating a chic and cozy space to gather. Five windows in a row offer a breathtaking view of the sea. The apartment is equipped with two independent bathrooms. To make the legendary hotel that is La Ponche, a place just for you.
