ESSENTIAL ADDRESSES

La Ponche shares its hidden addresses and secret spots with its friends. So that every traveler can discover Saint-Tropez like a Tropezian. Ask the concierge where to go out at the moment, you will be surprised. Or go directly to the hotel's City Guide.

Plage Restaurant

CHEZ CAMILLE

In Ramatuelle, Chez Camille is still one of the true restaurants serving authentic family cuisine. On the menu is the legendary bouillabaisse, recognized as the house specialty. But also, fresh fish and lobsters from the Mediterranean, grilled over a wood fire...

Address : Résidence Cap Nioulargue, 83350 Ramatuelle

Restaurant

SENEQUIER

Two steps away from the hotel, recognizable among all, overlooking the port, the terrace of the Senequier brasserie is all dressed in red and serves coffee in all forms: frappés, liégeois with ice cream and chocolate, and viennois with cream, since 1930... Boutique next door, at the well-known Place aux Herbes.

Address : 29 Quai Jean Jaurès, 83990 Saint-Tropez

Plage Restaurant

GIGI

Gigi beach is more than a restaurant; this beach house is a real place of life. Come and enjoy the day, where everything is done to relax, share, and create lasting memories. Swimming pool, pool bar, private tents, kids club, wellness areas...

Address : Résidence Cap Nioulargue, 83350 Ramatuelle

Restaurant

LA SAUVAGEONNE

Come and discover this driftwood hut hidden in a nest of bamboo. The atmosphere is festive and offbeat in the middle of lush vegetation. Enjoy a refined and authentic Mediterranean cuisine with barbecued meats and fish, or desserts made to taste “just like home." With one look upward, you are dining under the stars.

Address : 396 Route de Bonne Terrasse 83350 Ramatuelle

Restaurant

LE TIGRR

Le TIGrr is one of the best-kept secrets of the French Riviera. It offers the best dishes of Thai and Asian cuisine with an exceptional panoramic view of the Gulf of Saint-Tropez. For a total change of scenery, the bar-restaurant offers a turquoise-colored Perroquet cocktail. As for the cuisine, Le TIGrr’s restaurant menu offers sushi, egg rolls, summer rolls, Thai-style bass, and more.

Address : 2-6 Rue de la Rampe, 83990 Saint-Tropez

The Beaches

BEACH LES SALINS

This white sand beach extends over 500 meters in a natural setting. Even in season, you can find a place for your towel. On the left, by the coastal path, you can reach a small cove that is calmer, especially in summer.

Address : 14 Avenue Paul Signac 83990 Saint-Tropez

The Beaches

BEACH DES GRANIERS

No need for a car, the beach is nestled a few steps from the citadel. Access is just as easy by boat: drop anchor, a shuttle is at your disposal! You can now enjoy the elegant setting of the beach and its amenities before having lunch with your feet in the sand.

Address : Plage des Graniers 83990 Saint-Tropez

Vignoble

Château Léoube

Château Léoube is one of the most beautiful wine estates in the Var. Between the paradisiacal seaside and the Massif des Maures mountain range, Château Léoube is known for its organic wine, a little less for its olive oil, more and more for its coffee. Feet in the sand, with a view of Pellegrin Beach.

Address : 2387 Route de Léoube, 83230 Bormes-les-Mimosas

Vignoble

Château Minuty

Château Minuty, located on the peninsula of Saint-Tropez for 80 years, holds over 170 hectares of vineyards. A great family history spanning several generations, which has made the estate exceptional. Visit and tasting by appointment. Ask our concierge.

Address : 2491 Route de la Berle, 83580 Gassin

Mode

Rondini Tropezian Shoes

The legendary flat sandals with four or five leather straps. These famous Tropezians have been handcrafted since 1927 by the Rondini family, the oldest craft house in Saint-Tropez...

Address : 18 Rue Georges Clemenceau, 83990 Saint-Tropez

Biscuiterie

biscuiterie de saint tropez

It offers a wide range of cookies, cakes, and also the famous soft white nougat, appreciated for its almonds and honey... No preservatives, no coloring, and no additives. The perfect gift for the return home.

Address : 3 Traverse des Calfats, 83990 Saint-Tropez

Fromagerie

fromagerie du marché

With a wide range of cheeses, this boutique offers goat cheese from Provence, Saint-Félix from Berry, Anneau from Vic-Bilh, Taupinière from Charentes, Pélardon from Cévennes, old Parmesan, and Tomme from Savoie; all chosen with art and matured to perfection, in an idyllic and fragrant shop.

Address : 16 Place aux Herbes, 83990 Saint-Tropez
